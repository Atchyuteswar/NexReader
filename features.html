build knowledge

03 · Cloud & Sync

Your library,
everywhere

Reading position, highlights, and notes sync across all your devices in under one second. Fully offline-capable — it works perfectly on a flight, in the mountains, or anywhere without signal.

  • Real-time WebSocket sync — under 1 second latency
  • Offline-first with CRDT conflict resolution
  • Encrypted per-user cloud storage (AES-256)
  • Resume where you left off on any device, instantly
  • Resumable uploads for large books and comic archives
  • 30-day recycle bin — deleted books always recoverable
