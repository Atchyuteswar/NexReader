Common Questions

Frequently asked

Can I keep my books if I cancel?
Yes — your books and annotations always belong to you. If you cancel, you keep everything you've downloaded locally. Cloud access reverts to the free plan limits (5 books, 1GB).
What formats can I bring in?
All 20+ supported formats: EPUB, PDF, MOBI, AZW, DOCX, CBZ, DJVU, FB2, TXT, Markdown, and more. You can also import directly from Google Drive, Dropbox, iCloud, OneDrive, or by URL.
Is my library private?
Completely. Your books are stored in a private, encrypted S3 bucket only you can access. AI features process text in ephemeral contexts and are never used for training. On-device AI never leaves your phone.
Does the AI work offline?
Basic AI features (vocabulary, on-device OCR) work fully offline. Cloud AI features like Ask the Book and summarisation require an internet connection — but reading position and annotations always sync when you reconnect.
Is there a student discount?
Yes — students with a verified university email get 40% off the Scholar plan. Contact us at students@nexreader.app with your institutional email to apply.
How does the free trial work?
Pro comes with a 14-day free trial — no credit card required. You get full access to all Pro features. After 14 days, you're automatically moved to the free plan unless you subscribe.
